Our History

Sri Surya Hardev Industries was founded in 1992 by Mr. Subba Rao Kurella. Since our inception, we have established ourselves as a leading presence in the rice milling industry across Andhra Pradesh.

We are proud to be the main dealers for renowned brands such as Garware Technical Fibers, Revo, Hindustan, and other major companies in the rice milling sector. Our extensive sub-dealer network spans the entirety of Andhra Pradesh, allowing us to serve a wide range of clients efficiently.

Our commitment to quality and service has earned us the trust of industry leaders. We are honored to count among our valued clients prestigious names such as Cargill Pvt Ltd, CP Aqua, Pattabhi Agros, Lalitha, and many others.

At Sri Surya Hardev Industries, we pride ourselves on offering competitive pricing across our entire product range, ensuring our customers receive the best value without compromising on quality.
