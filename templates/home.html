About Our Business

Established over three decades ago, Sri Surya Hardev Industries has built a vast customer network across Andhra Pradesh, Odisha, and Chhattisgarh. Our journey began with bag closing stitching machines, and we have since expanded our offerings to provide comprehensive solutions for rice mills and various industrial sectors.

Today, we pride ourselves on supplying a complete range of machinery and equipment, from A to Z, catering to the diverse needs of rice mills and other industrial applications. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our growth and success over the years.
